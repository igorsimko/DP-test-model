Reality Check[1] (ISBN 978-0-061-22766-0) is a book written by Peter Abrahams[2] and published on 28 April 2009 by HarperTeen (an imprint of HarperCollins),[3] going on to win the Edgar Award for Best Young Adult in 2010.[4]
