Judith Church (25 May 1944 in Beeston, Nottinghamshire, England – 2008 in Nottingham) was the author of two books on local history.[1]
Publications[edit]

Beeston Now And Then (2002: ISBN 0-9541485-0-9)
Beating the Invader: Beeston and Chilwell in World War Two and the Floods (2006: ISBN 0-9553849-0-7)
